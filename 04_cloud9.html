Cloud9 Standard is Free

Sign up for free Cloud9 developer workspace.

  • Click button SIGN UP FOR FREE at https://c9.io/site/pricing/

  • Enter your developer username

  • Enter your developer email address, and confirm it

  • Click button Create your free account

  • Read Welcome to Cloud9! Please activate your account! in your GMail

  • Click the link to activate your account

    Note

    Here is a smaple confirmation email:

  • In the resulting webpage, enter your developer password and confirm it

  • Your Cloud9 workspace will display at https://c9.io/ALT-username

  • Click the page title to set your full name

  • In the ADD-ON SERVICES area, click the activate link for Github.

  • A new github App Authorization window will appear. Click button Allow.

Congratulations. Now you have a Linux workspace in the cloud, accessible to you from any computer with a web browser which supports HTML5. Repositories you create or fork on GitHub will be available to you from menu selection PROJECTS ON GITHUB at the left of your Cloud9 desktop.
